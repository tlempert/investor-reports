Salesforce, 2014-2016. The fear: Slack and email were killing CRM. Salesforce compressed. What happened: Salesforce eventually bought Slack and became a broader enterprise platform than ever.

The pattern across all three: dominant workflow platforms with high net revenue retention survive disruption fears by absorbing the disruptor, not surrendering to it.
